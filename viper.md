# Privacy Policy

**Viper – Puzzle Snake Game**
**Effective Date: April 20, 2026**

## Overview

Viper ("the App") is a mobile puzzle game developed by Cesare Celozzi ("we", "us", "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how the App works with respect to your data.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.**

The App:
- Does not require an internet connection to function
- Does not collect personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- Does not collect device identifiers or location data
- Does not use analytics, crash reporting, or tracking services
- Does not contain advertisements
- Does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies

## Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data **locally on your device only** using SharedPreferences:
- Game progress (unlocked levels, star ratings)
- User preferences (audio volume, control mode, visual skin, speed)
- Custom levels created by the user

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is safe for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will revise the "Effective Date" at the top. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
